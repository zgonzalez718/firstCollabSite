About YearUp

Year Up is a non-profit organization whose mission is to close the opportunity divide. The opportunity divide is the talent shortge that makes it difficult for companies to fill positions. Year Up does this by using a high expectation high suport model. During the first six months of the year long program student are taught technical skills and professional skills. In the next six months student apply the classrom skills they learned at their internish with one of Year Up's corporate patners.
